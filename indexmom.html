Happy 40th birthday, Mama. I was scrambling to think about what to make you for this one. I always like to make you something personal, and every year something comes to mind, but this year was different. I was so wrapped in tests and school that I didn’t even realize how close we came to your birthday, and I was completely blank on what to give you for your most important birthday. Then I thought, and I thought for a long time, and I realized that the obvious answer is to make you something from the heart, and that is what this website is. It’s meant to be my personal gift to you. I might not be done completely by your birthday, but I will work on this till I think it’s done and I should be ready to give you something till your birthday. And don’t worry, I also have a store bought gift that I think you will love.

This is likely the most important birthday of your life, marking the halfway point of your life. People say time is like an hourglass, with the sand down representing the past and the sand up top representing the future, which is slowly shrinking. This is the only point in your life when the sand is equal, and from now on, there will be more sand in the bottom than the top. Half your life is over, but don’t worry; you still have another half to live. I guess that’s why people call 40 the new 0; it makes them feel younger, but it also represents that this is the point where you can restart. You can live life more in the present than the future because eventually, there will be no future left to worry about. So stop worrying about later and live in the present because life is meant to be lived forward and only makes sense backwards. So don’t worry about the future; don’t worry about the lemons life will throw at you; just remember, in the end, all will work out. All you have to do is make lemonade.
